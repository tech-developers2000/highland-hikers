Sandhan Valley is a popular trekking destination located in the Western Ghats of Maharashtra, India, specifically in the Sahyadri mountain range. It is situated in the Ahmednagar district, near the town of Samrad. The valley is well-known for its scenic beauty, challenging terrain, and unique features such as narrow canyons, rock formations, and lush greenery.
