    Sandhan Valley is a popular trekking destination located in the Western Ghats of Maharashtra, India, specifically in the Sahyadri mountain range. It is situated in the Ahmednagar district, near the town of Samrad. The valley is well-known for its scenic beauty, challenging terrain, and unique features such as narrow canyons, rock formations, and lush greenery.

    Day 1 : Departure and Scenic Exploration

    12:30 PM - Train Departure from CSMT
    • Board your train at Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus (CSMT) for a fun-packed adventure!
    12:43 PM - Train Departure from Dadar
    • Dadar passengers: Hop on at 12:43 PM.
    3:00 PM - Arrival at Kasara
    • Kasara Station: Your adventure begins!
    3:15 PM – Travel to Fog City (Igatpuri)
    • Departure to Fog City, Igatpuri (approx. 30 minutes by Toofan car).
    3:45 PM – Visit Bhavali Dam (Waterfall Stop)
    • Bhavali Dam: Scenic waterfall for a short stop and photo opportunities.
    4:30 PM – Arrive at Gavhande Waterfall
    • Enjoy the Gavhande Waterfall, a hidden gem with breathtaking views.
    5:45 PM – Reach AMK Plateau
    • AMK Plateau: Relax and engage in a friendly game of cricket while enjoying panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.
    6:30 PM – Arrive at Bhandardara Entry Gate
    • Prepare for the final leg of the journey to Ghatghar (Base Village).
    7:30 PM – Arrive at Ghatghar Base Village
    • Ghatghar Base Village: Settle into the campsite. Tents are ready for your overnight stay.
    8:00 PM – Campfire & Dinner
    • Campfire time: Enjoy the warmth of the campfire under the stars.
    • Dinner will be served to end the day on a delicious note.

    Day 2: Sandhan Valley Trek & Rappelling Adventure

    8:30 AM – Depart for Sandhan Valley
    • Breakfast: Enjoy Poha & Black Tea to fuel your morning.
    • Depart for Sandhan Valley, an exciting trek awaits!
    9:00 AM – Arrive at Sandhan Valley
    • Reach Sandhan Valley, known for its narrow gorges, unique rock formations, and thrilling trekking routes.
    9:15 AM - 12:00 PM – Trekking & Rappelling
    • Trek through the valley: Enjoy the rugged landscape and adventurous trek.
    • Rappelling: Experience the excitement of descending rock faces (weight limit up to 120 kg).
    12:00 PM – Complete the Trek
    • Finish your trekking and rappelling adventure by 12:00 PM.
    • Relax, hydrate, and take in the natural beauty.
    12:30 PM – Return to Base Village
    • Head back to Ghatghar Base Village for a brief rest.
    2:00 PM – Leave for Kasara
    • Depart Ghatghar for your return journey to Kasara.
    5:20 PM – Arrive at Kasara
    • Arrival at Kasara Station: Freshen up and relax before the journey back.
    6:17 PM – Train Departure from Kasara
    • Board the 6:17 PM train from Kasara to head back to Mumbai or your next destination.
