Adventure Trip Itinerary: Kasara to Sandhan Valley & Bhandardara

Day 1: Departure & Scenic Exploration

  • 12:30 PM: Train departs from CSMT, with a stop at Dadar at 12:43 PM.
  • 3:00 PM: Arrive at Kasara, then travel to Igatpuri (Fog City).
  • 3:45 PM: Visit Bhavali Dam for a waterfall stop.
  • 4:30 PM: Explore Gavhande Waterfall.
  • 5:45 PM: Relax at AMK Plateau with a cricket game.
  • 6:30 PM: Arrive at Bhandardara Entry Gate and head to Ghatghar Base Village.
  • 7:30 PM: Settle into the campsite and enjoy a campfire and dinner.

Day 2: Sandhan Valley Trek & Rappelling

  • 8:30 AM: Breakfast (Poha & Black Tea) and depart for Sandhan Valley.
  • 9:00 AM: Arrive at Sandhan Valley for trekking and rappelling.
  • 12:00 PM: Finish the trek and rappelling, then head back to Ghatghar.
  • 2:00 PM: Depart for Kasara.
  • 5:20 PM: Arrive at Kasara and relax before the return journey.
  • 6:17 PM: Train departs from Kasara.

Included:

  • Transportation: Travel by Toofan car between Kasara and Ghatghar.
  • Accommodation: Tent stay at Ghatghar.
  • Meals: Maggi & Tea, Dinner, Poha & Tea, and lunch after the trek.
  • Activities: Cricket at AMK Plateau, Rappelling in Sandhan Valley.
  • Additional: Campfire, tent accommodation.

Packing Tips:

  • Trekking shoes, warm clothing, water bottle, camera, flashlight, and personal essentials.

Travel Notes:

  • Moderate physical activity; ensure fitness for trekking and rappelling.
  • Pack for varying temperatures, especially cooler evenings.
